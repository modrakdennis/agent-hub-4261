## 1. Daily Prioritizer

I need help planning my day. Here is my list of tasks, deadlines, and any constraints:

[TASKS]

Please:
1. Rank them by impact and urgency.
2. Group into 'Do first', 'Schedule later', 'Delegate', and 'Drop'.
3. Suggest a realistic hour-by-hour schedule.
4. Point out any conflicts or dependencies I might have missed.

Be concise but specific.

---

## 2. Email Refiner

Rewrite the email below to make it clearer, more polite, and more professional. Keep my main point and tone, but fix grammar, shorten rambling sentences, and make the call to action obvious.

[DRAFT EMAIL]

Output the improved email only, ready to send. If anything is missing (subject line, recipient, deadline, attachment), note it in a short 'Before sending' checklist.

---

## 3. Explain Like I'm Five

Explain the topic below as if I am a smart 10-year-old. Use simple words, analogies, and one concrete example. Avoid jargon. Then add a one-line summary a busy adult could use.

[TOPIC]

---

## 4. Meeting Summarizer

Summarize the meeting notes or transcript below.

1. List decisions made.
2. List action items with owners and deadlines.
3. Flag any open questions or risks.
4. Suggest the one follow-up message I should send.

[MEETING NOTES / TRANSCRIPT]

---

## 5. Decision Framework

I am deciding between the options below. Help me think it through.

[OPTIONS]

For each option, list:
1. Pros
2. Cons
3. Best-case outcome
4. Worst-case outcome
5. One hidden assumption I might be making

Then recommend the best option and explain why. If you need more context to decide, ask me up to 3 clarifying questions.
